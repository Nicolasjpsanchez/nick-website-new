Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a beloved Indian dish that combines tender chicken pieces simmered in a creamy, spiced tomato-based sauce. This rich and flavorful curry is infused with aromatic spices like garam masala, cumin, and coriander, perfectly balanced with the velvety smoothness of butter and cream. Typically served with naan or basmati rice, Butter Chicken offers a luxurious dining experience that’s both comforting and indulgent. Whether for a special occasion or a cozy dinner at home, this dish is a timeless classic that never fails to impress.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz Chicken Breast
  • 6 tsp Plain Greek Yogurt
  • 2 tsp Turmeric
  • 2 tsp Chili Powder
  • 2 tsp Kashmiri Chili Powder
  • 4 tbsp Ginger Paste
  • 4 tbsp Garlic Paste

Directions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, Kashmiri chili powder, ginger paste, and garlic paste for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the ginger paste, garam masala, turmeric, cumin seeds, and chili powder. Stir until fragrant.
  4. Blend the tomatoes into a smooth paste and add them to the pan. Cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  5. Add the marinated chicken and cook until fully cooked.
  6. Serve hot with basmati rice or naan bread.
  7. Enjoy!
