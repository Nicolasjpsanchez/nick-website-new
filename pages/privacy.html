Privacy Policy

Understand how we handle your information and protect your privacy.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At nicolasjpsanchez.com, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you contact us via forms, we may collect your name, email address, and any other details you voluntarily provide.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interaction with the website, such as pages viewed, time spent on pages, and other diagnostic data.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and collect information about your preferences and usage patterns.

How We Use Your Information

  • To improve the website’s performance and user experience.
  • To respond to inquiries or feedback submitted through the contact form.
  • To analyze website usage for internal analytics and reporting.
  • To ensure the website’s security and protect against potential threats.

Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. However, we may share your data in the following cases:

  • With Service Providers: To help us operate the website and deliver services.
  • For Legal Compliance: If required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.

Cookies Policy

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. We use cookies to:

  • Recognize your device during future visits.
  • Understand your preferences and personalize your experience.
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to secure your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
