Spicy Pomegranate Moscow Mule

This Spicy Pomegranate Moscow Mule is a bold and refreshing cocktail that combines the tangy sweetness of pomegranate with a kick of spice. This modern twist on the classic Moscow Mule features fresh lime juice, spicy ginger beer, and a splash of pomegranate juice, creating a vibrant and flavorful drink. Garnished with pomegranate seeds and a slice of jalapeño for an extra zing, this cocktail is perfect for entertaining or enjoying a unique twist on a timeless favorite. Serve it chilled in a traditional copper mug for the ultimate experience.

Ingredients

Single Serving

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • Juice from 1/2 lime
  • Juice from 1/4 of a small grapefruit
  • 1/3 cup pomegranate juice
  • 1-2 jalapeño slices
  • Ginger beer, for topping
  • Pomegranate arils and fresh mint, for serving

Pitcher (Serves 4)

  • 1 cup (8 ounces) vodka
  • Juice of 2 limes (about 1/2 cup)
  • Juice of 1 small grapefruit (about 1/2 cup)
  • 1 1/3 cups pomegranate juice
  • 1 jalapeño, sliced
  • Ginger beer, for topping

Directions

Single Serving

  1. Fill a cocktail glass with ice.
  2. Combine vodka, lime juice, grapefruit juice, pomegranate juice, and jalapeños in a cocktail shaker. Shake until combined (about 1 minute).
  3. Strain into your prepared glass.
  4. Top with ginger beer and garnish with pomegranate arils and mint. Enjoy!

Pitcher

  1. Combine vodka, lime juice, grapefruit juice, pomegranate juice, and jalapeños in a large pitcher.
  2. Stir to combine and chill for 2 hours. The longer the jalapeños sit, the spicier the drink.
  3. Strain out the jalapeños and discard.
  4. When ready to serve, fill the pitcher with ice and top off with ginger beer, or serve in individual glasses topped with ginger beer.
